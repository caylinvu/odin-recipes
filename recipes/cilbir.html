Cilbir (Turkish Eggs)

Description

This is one of my favorite recipes to make ever!!! It has a tangy and garlicky greek yogurt base, topped with poached eggs and a chili butter sauce, and served with a side of toast. Delicious!!!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 1 lemon
  • at least 2 or 3 garlic cloves
  • smoked paprika
  • red pepper flakes
  • chili oil
  • butter
  • sourdough bread
  • fresh dill

Steps

  1. Boil large pot of water. While waiting for water to boil, zest lemon, mince garlic, and chop dill. Mix greek yogurt with lemon zest and minced garlic and set aside.
  2. Melt 1-2 tablespoons of butter on medium-low heat on a skillet. Add about a teaspoon of chili oil, some red pepper flakes, and a good amount of smoked paprika. Set aside.
  3. Once water is boiling, poach 2 eggs and set aside to cool.
  4. Butter each side of a piece of sourdough and toast on a skillet.
  5. Spread yogurt mixture on a plate and top with poached eggs, chili butter, and chopped dill. Serve with side of toast.
